Vehicle Detection and Counting

Counting vehicles using Yolo11, OpenCV & ByteTrack



A snippet of the vehicle detection, tracking, and counting process. I have assigned a distinct color to each of the 7 vehicle classes. Individual vehicle counts are displayed at the top-left of the frame. Tracking is handled by ByteTrack, which is incredibly reliable in tracking vehicles in congested scenarios. While the model occasionally struggles with pedestrians and bikes (both bicycles and motorbikes) due to low image quality, it performs much better than the default COCO-trained model in detecting bikes.

I am still refining the dataset to further improve its accuracy. I also plan to explore extracting traffic metrics from these video footages, such as turning movements, vehicle speeds, delay, saturation flow etc.
